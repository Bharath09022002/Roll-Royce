About Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce is a luxury automobile manufacturer known for producing some of the most prestigious and customizable vehicles in the world. Founded in 1904, Rolls-Royce has become synonymous with elegance, craftsmanship, and advanced technology.

The brand's commitment to quality and innovation has solidified its reputation as one of the leading luxury car manufacturers. Rolls-Royce vehicles are powered by advanced V12 engines, delivering smooth yet powerful performance. The brand's signature "Magic Carpet Ride" suspension system ensures an exceptionally smooth ride.

Notable Features

Rolls-Royce cars are known for several unique features that set them apart from other luxury vehicles:

  • Starlight Headliner: A unique feature that incorporates thousands of fiber-optic lights to replicate a starry night sky in the car's ceiling.
  • Spirit of Ecstasy: The iconic hood ornament that can be customized to reflect the owner's taste.
  • Bespoke Options: Rolls-Royce offers a wide range of customization options, allowing owners to tailor every aspect of their vehicle, from paint colors to interior materials.
  • Advanced Technology: Rolls-Royce integrates the latest technology in their vehicles, including advanced infotainment systems and driver-assistance features.

Recent Models

The new Phantom at "The Great Eight Phantoms Exhibit" is the current Flagship Model and the most expensive production car made by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. It is available in two lengths: a standard version and an Extended Wheelbase version, which adds 200mm of length for enhanced rear passenger comfort.

In May 2022, Rolls-Royce unveiled the Series II Phantom, featuring subtle exterior changes and four additional wheel options, reflecting the brand's dedication to continuous innovation while retaining its classic elegance.
